Zogota is a town in southeastern Guinea . Mining [ edit ] 

It is the site of large iron ore deposits. [1] [2] Initial production proposed for 2012 is 2mtpa. The mine is owned by BSG Resources . [3] [ unreliable source? ] 

The mine will be connected by a standard gauge railway across the border to a port in Liberia .
