حصارک District 

Hesarak District, Nangarhar Province. Country Afghanistan Province Nangarhar Province Capital Hesarak Population (2002 [1] ) • Total 28,462 Time zone Afghanistan Standard Time ( UTC+4:30 ) 

Hesarak or Hisarak ( Pashto : حصارک,| Dari ولسوالی حصارک is a district in the west of Nangarhar Province , Afghanistan . Its population was estimated at 28,462 in 2002. The district centre is the village of Hesarak . References [ edit ] ^ MRRD Provincial profile for Nangarhar Province UNHCR District Profile , dated 2002-07-02, accessed 2006-07-14 (PDF).
