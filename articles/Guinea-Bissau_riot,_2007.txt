Guinea-Bissau riot, 2007 From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation , search 

The Guinea-Bissau riot of 2007 was a protest by Bissauan civilians in the Bairro Militar District of the capital, Bissau . The district is a suburb of the city. It was staged against the murder of former Navy chief, Mohamed Lamine Sanha . The police opened fire on the protesters, killing one man.
